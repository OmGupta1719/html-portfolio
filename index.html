The Best Movies According To Me

My Top 3 Movies

A Quiet Place

The story is set in a post-apocalyptic world where the Earth has been invaded by blind extraterrestrial creatures with hypersensitive hearing. These creatures hunt by sound and are extremely deadly.

How To Train Your Dragon

The story takes place in the mythical Viking world of Berk, where dragons are seen as vicious creatures that constantly raid the village and cause destruction. The protagonist, Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III, is a young Viking who dreams of becoming a dragon slayer like his father, Stoick the Vast, who is the chief of the village.

Five Feet Apart

The story follows Stella, a bright and organized teenager who has spent a significant portion of her life in the hospital due to her CF. She strictly follows her treatment routine to manage her condition. Stella meets Will, a rebellious and artistic young man with CF, who is participating in a clinical trial for a new drug.
